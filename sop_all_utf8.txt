Guideline Category,Best Practice,Example/Description,,
Language & Tone,"Use active voice, present tense, imperative mood","""Click the Submit button"" instead of ""The Submit button should be clicked""",,
Clarity,"Use simple, concise language; avoid jargon unless defined","""Start the machine"" not ""Initiate operational commencement of equipment""",,
Consistency,Use same terms throughout; maintain uniform formatting,"If using ""employee"" don't switch to ""staff"" or ""personnel""",,
Specificity,"Include exact details: values, times, quantities, locations","""Heat to 350°F for 15 minutes"" not ""Heat until warm""",,
Action Orientation,Start each step with action verb,"""Verify"", ""Complete"", ""Submit"", ""Review"", ""Approve""",,
Numbering,Use hierarchical numbering for main steps and sub-steps,"1.0, 1.1, 1.2, 2.0, 2.1 or 1, 1a, 1b, 2, 2a",,
Visual Aids,"Include screenshots, diagrams, flowcharts for complex steps",Annotated screenshots showing where to click,,
Page Layout,"Use headers/footers with SOP ID, page number, effective date","""SOP-HR-001 | Page 3 of 12 | Effective: 01-Jan-2025""",,
Conditional Steps,Clearly mark IF-THEN scenarios and decision points,"""IF error code appears, THEN contact IT Support""",,
Time References,Specify exact timeframes and deadlines,"""Within 24 hours"", ""By end of business day"", ""15-minute intervals""",,
Measurements,Use standard units with precision,"""250ml ±5ml"", ""Room temperature (20-25°C)""",,
Safety Emphasis,"Use WARNING, CAUTION, NOTE boxes for critical information",WARNING: High voltage - disconnect power before maintenance,,
Cross-References,"Link to related SOPs, policies, forms","""Refer to SOP-QA-005 for quality inspection procedure""",,
Version Control,"Clearly mark version, highlight changes from previous version","""Version 3.2 - Updated Section 4.5 (equipment list)""",,

Mistake Category,Specific Error,Impact,How to Avoid,Example
Scope Issues,Scope too broad or unclear,Confusion about when to use SOP,Define clear boundaries: what's included and excluded,"""Customer Service"" vs ""Handling Tier-1 Phone Support Calls"""
Language Problems,Using passive voice,Unclear who performs action,"Use active voice: ""The manager approves"" not ""approval is given""","""The form is submitted"" → ""Submit the form"""
Vague Instructions,"Using ambiguous terms like ""appropriate"", ""sufficient""","Inconsistent execution, quality issues","Specify exact criteria, measurements, quantities","""Add appropriate amount"" → ""Add 250ml ±5ml"""
Missing Prerequisites,Not stating required knowledge or access,Unqualified people attempting process,"List all training, certifications, system access required",Assuming everyone knows how to use the ERP system
Outdated Content,SOP doesn't reflect current process,"People ignore SOP, compliance issues","Set regular review schedule, assign owner",Still referencing old software version
No Visual Aids,Long text without diagrams or screenshots,"Difficult to understand, training takes longer","Add flowcharts for complex logic, screenshots for software",10 paragraphs describing software navigation
Poor Organization,"Random order, illogical sequence","Users can't find information, time wasted","Follow standard template, use table of contents",Safety information buried in middle of document
Missing Decision Points,"No guidance on ""what if"" scenarios",Process stops when exceptions occur,"Include troubleshooting, escalation procedures","No instruction for ""If payment fails..."""
Over-Complication,Including unnecessary detail or edge cases,"SOP too long, users overwhelmed","Focus on 80/20 rule, put rare scenarios in appendix",Documenting every possible error message
No Metrics,No way to measure success or effectiveness,"Can't improve, can't prove compliance","Define KPIs, set targets, require documentation",No tracking of processing time or error rate
Single Format Only,"Using only one format (e.g., text only)",Doesn't match process complexity,Choose format based on process needs,Using simple list for complex decision tree
No Ownership,No one responsible for SOP accuracy,"SOP becomes outdated, no one to ask questions",Assign process owner with clear accountability,"Generic ""HR Department"" instead of specific role"
Missing References,No links to related documents or policies,"Duplicate info, inconsistency across docs","Cross-reference related SOPs, policies, regulations",Repeating company policy instead of referencing it
No Training Plan,Publishing SOP without training users,"SOP not followed, defeats the purpose","Develop training program, assess competency",Emailing SOP without explanation or training
Rigid Structure,No provision for exceptions or variations,Process breaks in real situations,"Include escalation process, emergency procedures","""Must always call customer"" even if unreachable"
Poor Version Control,"Multiple versions circulating, unclear which is current","People follow wrong version, compliance risk","Use document management system, clear version numbering",3 different versions in circulation
No Feedback Loop,No way for users to suggest improvements,"Process doesn't improve, user frustration","Establish feedback mechanism, regular review meetings",No channel for frontline staff to report issues
Legal Gaps,Missing regulatory requirements or compliance steps,"Legal violations, fines, audit failures",Involve legal/compliance team in review,Missing GDPR consent step in data collection
Safety Oversights,Inadequate safety warnings or PPE requirements,"Injuries, liability, OSHA violations","Involve safety officer, highlight all hazards","Missing ""Wear safety goggles"" for chemical handling"
No Change Management,SOP updated without managing transition,"Confusion during changeover, errors spike","Plan transition, communicate changes, phase implementation",New version goes live with no announcement

=== SHEET: SOP Writing Guidelines ===

Guideline Category,Best Practice,Example/Description,Common Mistakes to Avoid
Language & Tone,"Use active voice, present tense, imperative mood","""Click the Submit button"" instead of ""The Submit button should be clicked""","Passive voice, future tense, ambiguous phrasing"
Clarity,"Use simple, concise language; avoid jargon unless defined","""Start the machine"" not ""Initiate operational commencement of equipment""","Technical jargon without definitions, overly complex sentences"
Consistency,Use same terms throughout; maintain uniform formatting,"If using ""employee"" don't switch to ""staff"" or ""personnel""","Inconsistent terminology, mixed formatting styles"
Specificity,"Include exact details: values, times, quantities, locations","""Heat to 350°F for 15 minutes"" not ""Heat until warm""","Vague instructions like ""some"", ""many"", ""appropriate"""
Action Orientation,Start each step with action verb,"""Verify"", ""Complete"", ""Submit"", ""Review"", ""Approve""","Starting with ""The user should..."" or ""It is necessary to..."""
Numbering,Use hierarchical numbering for main steps and sub-steps,"1.0, 1.1, 1.2, 2.0, 2.1 or 1, 1a, 1b, 2, 2a","Non-sequential numbering, mixed formats"
Visual Aids,"Include screenshots, diagrams, flowcharts for complex steps",Annotated screenshots showing where to click,Text-only explanations for visual processes
Page Layout,"Use headers/footers with SOP ID, page number, effective date","""SOP-HR-001 | Page 3 of 12 | Effective: 01-Jan-2025""","Missing page numbers, no document identification"
Conditional Steps,Clearly mark IF-THEN scenarios and decision points,"""IF error code appears, THEN contact IT Support""","Ambiguous decision criteria, unclear branching"
Time References,Specify exact timeframes and deadlines,"""Within 24 hours"", ""By end of business day"", ""15-minute intervals""","""As soon as possible"", ""promptly"", ""timely manner"""
Measurements,Use standard units with precision,"""250ml ±5ml"", ""Room temperature (20-25°C)""","""A little"", ""approximately"", ""about"""
Safety Emphasis,"Use WARNING, CAUTION, NOTE boxes for critical information",WARNING: High voltage - disconnect power before maintenance,Burying safety information in regular text
Cross-References,"Link to related SOPs, policies, forms","""Refer to SOP-QA-005 for quality inspection procedure""",Repeating information instead of referencing
Version Control,"Clearly mark version, highlight changes from previous version","""Version 3.2 - Updated Section 4.5 (equipment list)""","No change tracking, unclear what was modified"
